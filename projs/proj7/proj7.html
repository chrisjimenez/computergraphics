Playing with the HTML5 Canvas

I used the same rod idea that was covered in class except I made smaller rods that covers the inner canvas. I looped through the inner canvas to set up a spinning rod at every other space(space in this case is 10 pixels). The canvas is interactive with the mouse, so if you click in the canvas, the rods will rotate with respect to the mouse location. This creates a really cool effect where the rods act like a piece of cloth. Try it out!

For this experiment I was trying to mimic a wave motion using circles. This was a very simple implementation, basically looping through the inner canvas and creating a circle with a radius dependent on midpoint of the canvas. that is, the farther the circle is from the midpoint, the smaller the ir radii becomes. But you can see that as you get farther from the midpoint, the circles begin to increase in radii again.
